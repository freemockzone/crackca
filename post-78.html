Manipur Preparing for Special Intensive Revision (SIR)

Key Highlights

  • SIR in Manipur
    • Training and Meetings are underway:
      • Booth-Level Officers (BLOs) and supervisors are being trained.
      • Political parties have been briefed by the Joint Chief Electoral Officer (CEO), Ramananda Nongmeikapam.
    • No timeline yet announced for Manipur, though the qualifying date is 1.1.2026.
    • Activities are part of pre-revision processes, including:
      • House-to-house surveys.
      • Rationalisation of polling stations.
  • Concerns by Political Parties
    • Manipur Pradesh Congress Committee (MPCC) President Keisham Meghachandra:
      • Opposed the idea of changing voting addresses of internally displaced persons (IDPs) to their temporary relief camp locations.
      • Warned it may further disrupt peace and legitimize displacement.
