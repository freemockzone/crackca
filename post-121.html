Battery Waste Management & EPR Floor Price Issue

Source: The Hindu | Publication Date: 2025-08-05

Why in the News?

The Hindu newspaper article highlights a critical problem in India's battery waste management: the EPR (Extended Producer Responsibility) floor price is too low. This issue risks undermining the government's 2022 Battery Waste Management Rules (BWMR) and India's broader decarbonization and circular economy goals. The article points out that without a fair price, legitimate recyclers are unable to operate sustainably, leading to the proliferation of informal and fraudulent players who dump hazardous waste.

6. Conclusion

Effectively managing battery waste is not just an environmental issue; it is a strategic and economic imperative for India.
